Student Health: A Case Study of Portugal

Education is a vital aspect of society. I'm interested in the state of education and student health around the world. As a college student myself, I know that there are a lot of external factors that affect one’s study habits as well as their inclination to use alcohol as a possible coping mechanism for dealing with stress. Let's explore a dataset of 395 students attending one of two Portuguese secondary schools, Gabriel Pereira and Mousinho da Silveira.
