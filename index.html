Student Health: A Case Study of Portugal

Peter Regalbuto



Project Description: This project will explore a dataset of 395 Portuguese students attending one of two secondary schools. The project aims to answer the following questions:
    1) What is the distribution of all students in the dataset by age?
    2) How do parents’ education levels correlate with how much their children study?
    3) How are students balancing their time between relaxing, hanging out with friends, and spending time with family?
    4) How does students’ alcohol consumption compare with how they self-rate their health?
I am aiming to learn more about some external factors that may be affecting students and the methods they use to cope with internal and external stressors.
